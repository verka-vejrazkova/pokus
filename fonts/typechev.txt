[ English description ]

Digitalized characters typed with Czech version of the Chevron Deluxe typewriter.
I intentionally kept all flaws caused by its textile ribbon and different force of keypresses.
The font includes all original characters and I also created some missing characters that are part of the basic ASCII set.
